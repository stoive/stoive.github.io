Semantic elements

Script and style elements no longer require language or type attributes. Name me a stylesheet that isn't CSS, or a script that isn't JavaScript? 

<script>
	// here goes your JavaScript!
</script>
<style>
	/* here goes your CSS! */
</style>

Nothing exciting, but at least it's clean. There's also some niceties which save you from having too many cases of <div class="something">:

header, footer, article, section, nav,
aside, hgroup, time, mark

...which might help with SEO and accessibility one day.

What's more exciting, is rich snippets, which make use of microdata, microformats, and RDFa. These are currently used by Google to display more detailed search results.
